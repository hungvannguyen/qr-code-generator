Step 02

Enter the Details

Fill in the required fields for the type of QR code you are creating. This might include entering a link, contact information, or text. After filling in the details, click "Generate" to create the code.

Step 03

Customize a QR code

choose a QR code generator with customization features. Input the desired data (e.g., URL or text) and select options to modify the code's appearance. QR codes are incredibly versatile and can store a wide range of information. This includes URLs to open websites instantly, contact details like name, phone number, and email, as well as plain text messages. They can even store Wi-Fi credentials, making it easy for customers to connect to your network with a simple scan. The possibilities are endless for what can be embedded in a QR code.

Yes, it is possible to track QR code scans, including where, when, and how often they are scanned. Additionally, you can see which devices and operating systems were used for the scan. This data is valuable for measuring the success of marketing campaigns and understanding user behavior.

Static QR codes do not expire. They will remain functional indefinitely, as long as the information they contain (e.g., a URL) remains unchanged. If you want more flexibility, such as the ability to update content or track scans, you can opt for dynamic QR codes. These codes are also permanent but allow content to be modified after generation.

Static QR codes are fixed once created and cannot be edited or tracked. They are best for simple applications where the content doesn’t need to change. Dynamic QR codes, on the other hand, are more flexible and offer the ability to update the embedded content (such as links or text) after printing. Dynamic codes also allow for tracking and analytics, making them ideal for marketing campaigns.

After signing up for a QR code generator, you can use it to create and manage both static and dynamic QR codes.
